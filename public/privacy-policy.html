Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to HealthHaven! We are committed to safeguarding your privacy while promoting health and wellness in our community. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our platform. By using HealthHaven, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Your name and email address when creating an account
  • Username and profile information you choose to share
  • Health interests, preferences, and any content you submit on the platform

2.2 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

HealthHaven uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the platform. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to improve the platform and personalize your experience, including:

  • Providing, maintaining, and enhancing the HealthHaven community
  • Customizing your experience based on your preferences

  • Ensuring compliance with legal obligations and protecting the safety of our users

4. How We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted service providers who help operate the platform.
  • For Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect HealthHaven and its users.
  • In Case of Business Transfers: Your information may be transferred in the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring.

5. Your Choices

You control your personal information. You may update or delete your profile at any time and opt-out of communications by adjusting your settings. Please note that deleting your account will remove your content from the platform.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to protect your account by using a strong password and keeping your login credentials private.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

HealthHaven may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy regularly. Your continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
